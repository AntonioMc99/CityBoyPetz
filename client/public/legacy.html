Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe for kids to handle the animals?

Yes. Safety and hygiene are our top priorities. We guide all interactions and use only show-approved animals.

How much space do you need?

A 10×10 area is perfect. We bring everything we need; just provide an outlet and a table if possible.

Do you travel to my area?

We travel across the metro. A small travel fee may apply for longer distances—ask when booking.
